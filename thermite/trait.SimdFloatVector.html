unsafe fn to_int_fast(self) -> Self::Vi[src]

Can convert to a signed integer faster than a regular cast, but may not provide correct results above a certain range.

For example, f64 -> i64 is only valid from (-2^51, 2^51) or so.

On instruction sets where this can be done in-hardware, the results should be exact, but do not rely on this for large floats.

unsafe fn to_uint_fast(self) -> Self::Vu[src]

Can convert to an unsigned integer faster than a regular cast, but may not provide correct results above a certain range.

For example, f64 -> u64 is only valid from [0, 2^52) or so.

On instruction sets where this can be done in-hardware, the results should be exact, but do not rely on this for large floats.
